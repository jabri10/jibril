The Five Pillars of Islam

The Five Pillars of Islam are the foundation of a Muslim's faith and practice. They represent the core beliefs and practices that shape the life of every Muslim.

  • Shahada - The declaration of faith.
  • Salah - The five daily prayers.
  • Zakat - Charity and helping those in need.
  • Sawm - Fasting during the month of Ramadan.
  • Hajj - The pilgrimage to Mecca.
